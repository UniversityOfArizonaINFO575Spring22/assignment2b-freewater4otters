ONE-PLAYER MIN., COOPERATIVE

About HORRIFIED

In this game, characters with vastly different abilities must work together to eradicate iconic night creatures from a small town. This is a game of strategy, communication, and cooperation from all heroes in order to save themselves from the ghoulish Universal monsters--Dracula, Creature from the Black Lagoon, Frankenstein monster and Bride, The Mummy, and the Invisible Man. This game has various level of difficulty, making it the players choice how many monsters at minimum haunt and stalk the townspeople that inhabit the board game. Each creature has their own unique ability and instructions how to defeat them. This is a great game to play yearly but even more special during the month of October. This is a great co-op game with a maximum of five (5) players but can be played on solo mode, albeit, challenging! This is suitable for ages ten (10) and up, and it takes approximately sixty (60) minutes to complete.
