About PARKS, TRAILS, AND MORE

When there is a lull between, work, social, and general obligations, there is a certain activity that is both distracting and challenging to occupy the quiet moment of freedom. This, of course, are tabletop games, board games, cards games, whatever you may refer to popular stables like Operation or the gut-wrenching Monopoly. PARKS, TRAILS, AND MORE is an ode to this beloved pass time, and it will showcase some of my favorite games, including the peaceful and beautifully created Parks and Trails, a game designed around the United States National Parks featuring various artist interpretations of these worldly landscapes.
