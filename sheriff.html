About SHERIFF OF NOTTINGHAM

In this 2016 board game, players are merchants attempting to deliver goods to the market. Players take turns assuming the role of the Sheriff, who must decide which merchants' bags to inspect and which to let by. As a merchant, the goal is to convince the Sheriff to let you in- by any means possible. And the merchant with the most wealth wins! Players can take the easy route to gaining wealth by only delivering legal goods (e.g. bread, chicken, and apples), or smuggling higher priced illicit goods. This game puts a player's ability to bluff, persuade, and negotiate to the test, and not everyone can triumph smuggling contraband. Sheriff of Nottingham is the perfect party game with five players and appropriate for audiences 14 and older, with a completion time of sixty (60) minutes.
