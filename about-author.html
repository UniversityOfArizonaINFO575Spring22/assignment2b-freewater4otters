ABOUT THE CREATOR

At this point in time, I am pursuing a graduate degree for Library and Information Science, a fancy, expensive paper to qualified as a librarian at most information institutions. When I am not busy oscillating between school and my part-time job, I like to relax by taking naps--an activity I learned from grad school that stands against capitalism as it stops me from participating in the fast-paced environment. And when I'm not resting my eyes, I like to disconnect from technology and play tabletop games. So I dedicate this assignment to my favorite hobby.
