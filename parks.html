PARKS

Hike through National Park tiles, collecting memories and admiring gorgeous scenary - Keymaster Games

ONE-PLAYER MIN., COMPETITIVE

About PARKS

PARKS takes players across different trails across the four seasons of a year. Each trail represents a different season, and as each season passes, the trails change and grow steadily longer. Each player takes on the role of two hikers trekking the trail and while on the trail, hikers will see the sites and complete actions described on the tile. As hikers reach the end of the trail, hikers can exchange tokens picked up on the tiles to visit a National Park and earn points. The goal of the game is to have the most points from the Parks collected, bonuses, and photos taken on the trek through sites. This forty (40) to seventy (70) minute game can be adjusted to be played on a solo trek, or up to five players and suitable for players 10 and older. All the National Park tile featured art comes from the Fifty-Nine Parks Print Series and beautifully represent the natural parks surrounding the U.S.
